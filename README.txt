== Description ==

**Chord Connect.**  

A Video Community for your entire team on your own website.
Experience the highest quality video chat, no downloads required. Just send your personal video link to friends and family, and you‚Äôre off!

== Installation ==

**INSTALL Chord Connect FROM WITHIN WORDPRESS**  

1. Visit the plugins page within your dashboard and 
   select **Add New**
2. Search for **Chord Connect**
3. Click the **Install Now** button and wait until it 
   says **Activate**
4. Click **Activate**  


**INSTALL Chord Connect MANUALLY**  

1. Go to <a href="https://wordpress.org/plugins/chord-connect/">**https://wordpress.org/plugins/chord-connect/**</a>
2. Download the plugin and extract the **Chord Connect** folder from the .zip file.
3. Upload the **Chord Connect** folder to the **/wp-content/plugins/** directory
4. Activate the **Chord Connect** plugin through the **Plugins** menu in the WordPress dashboard  

== Frequently Asked Questions ==

**How can I invite specific guests to a meeting?**  

    1. Go to your personalized Chord Connect URL.
    2. Click on the ‚ÄòConnect‚Äô Tab on the right panel.
    3. Enter in one or more guests.  

For each guest you can:  

> ‚ÄúInvite by Mobile‚Äù in which case they will receive an invite via SMS text.
> ‚ÄúInvite by Email‚Äù in which case they will receive an invite via email.
Click the green phone icon to join the meeting and wait for guests to arrive.  

**How can I hold an open meeting?**  

    1. Go to your personalized Chord Connect URL.
    2. Click on the ‚ÄòSettings‚Äô Tab on the right panel and 
       make sure ‚ÄúOpen Room‚Äù is selected.
    3. Simply send your personalized Chord Connect URL via 
       email, text, calendar invite.
    4. Click the green phone icon to join the meeting and 
       wait for guests to arrive.  

**How do I invite chat with someone in my room?**  

You can send text messages to any individual in a room or to the whole group.
Within your room, click the Chat icon on the bottom left corner.
Select an individual to send a private message or ‚Äúeveryone‚Äù to message all of the participants.  

**How do I control who can access my room?**  

* There are four main access controls for your room. You will see an indicator at the top of the screen to show whether the room is open or closed.  

> **Open Room**
Any visitor who lands on your page will immediately join the Meeting. You do not have to be available or even present for them to do so.  

> **Open Room with PIN**
Visitors will have to verify themselves via a PIN. Once they have verified themselves, they will immediately join the Meeting. You do not have to be available or even present for them to do so.  

> **Closed Room**
If you are available, visitors who land on your page will have to ‚Äúcall‚Äù you in order to be accepted into the Meeting. If you are unavailable or away, they have the option to leave you a message.  

> **Closed Room with PIN**
Visitors will have to verify themselves via a PIN. Once they have verified themselves, and you are available, they can ‚Äúcall‚Äù you in order to be accepted into the Meeting. If you are unavailable or away, they have the option to leave you a message.  

**How can I control my audio settings?**  

* You can mute or unmute yourself at any time by clicking the microphone icon next to your avatar or at the bottom of the screen.
* You can also adjust any audio settings, such as choosing which microphone you want to use, by clicking the up arrow to the left of the microphone icon on the bottom of the screen.  

**How can I control my video settings?**  

* You can turn on or off your web camera at any time by clicking the camera icon next to your avatar or at the bottom of the screen.
* You can also adjust any video settings, such as choosing which camera you want to use, by clicking the up arrow to the right of the camera icon on the bottom of the screen.  

**How can I share my screen?**  

* If one or more participants is in the room any guest can share their screen, by clicking the ‚ÄúShare Screen‚Äù icon on the bottom left.
* A window will pop up asking you which screen to share.
* If you have multiple monitors you can choose to share which monitor you want to share.
* Or you can share a specific window such as a browser window or an application
* While sharing your screen a small bar will show reminding you that you are sharing your screen. You can stop sharing at any time by clicking the ‚ÄúStop Sharing‚Äù button.  

**Why am I not seeing the share screen or share video icons?**  

There must be at least two participants in a room for these icons to appear.  

**How can I share a video?**  

    1. If one or more participants is in the room any guest 
       can share a video (from YouTube or Vimeo), by 
       clicking the ‚ÄúVideo‚Äù icon on the bottom left.
    2. A window will pop up where you can paste the YouTube 
       or Vimeo URL. Then click ‚ÄúShare‚Äù.
    3. Once the video is shared you can control the video 
       using all of the video‚Äôs normal control (Play, Pause, 
       etc.)  

To stop sharing the video, just click the green ‚ÄúStop Video‚Äù icon on the bottom left.  

**How can I send text messages?**  

    1. Click the ‚ÄúChat‚Äù icon on the bottom left.
    2. Choose whether the person you want to send a private 
       message to or click ‚ÄúEveryone‚Äù to send a public message to the whole group.  

You can close the chat window by clicking the green icon on the bottom left.  

**How often is Chord Connect updated?**  

We will make periodic updates based on feedback we get from our users.
